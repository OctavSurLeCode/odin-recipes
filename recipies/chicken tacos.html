Chicken Tacos

These chicken tacos are packed with flavor and are perfect for a quick and delicious weeknight meal. Tender, seasoned chicken is paired with your favorite taco toppings for a satisfying dish everyone will love.

For 4 servings:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, heat the olive oil. Add the diced chicken and cook until browned and cooked through, about 6-8 minutes. Sprinkle the taco seasoning over the chicken and stir to combine. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  2. Warm the tortillas according to package instructions.
  3. Assemble the tacos by placing a spoonful of the chicken mixture onto each tortilla. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheddar cheese, diced onion, and chopped cilantro.
  4. Serve with lime wedges for squeezing over the tacos.
